\question[20] Answer the following questions:
\begin{parts}
	\part List all of the possible ways to produce an electric field
	\vspace{3cm}
	\part List all of the possible ways to produce a magnetic field
	\vspace{3cm}
	\part How does the phenomenon of ``light'' relate to the electric and magnetic fields?
	\vspace{3cm}
	\part We have seen on several occasions that electrons in circuits travel very slowly. Why do lights turn on almost instantly?
	\vspace{3cm}
	\part You rub a neutrally charged PVC rod with a neutrally charged fur cloth, and find that the PVC rod becomes negatively charged. Give a qualitative explanation of what has happened. Where did the charge come from? Does the fur cloth now have excess charge?
\end{parts}
